Our Story

A Farm Built
on a Dream

It started with a simple question: what if there was a place where the city felt a thousand miles away, yet home was never too far?

Sanjivani Farm was born out of that longing. Nestled in the quiet heartland of Palghar, our 16 acres were shaped by patience, care, and a deep love for the land. Every corner here tells a story — from the 700 coconut trees standing tall to the private lakes that mirror a sky you rarely get to see.

We are just over an hour from Mumbai, yet here you will feel something rare: stillness. Come as you are, and leave as you were always meant to feel.
