The Beginning

Nestled in Palghar's
Quiet Heartland

Sanjivani Farm was born out of that longing. Nestled in the quiet heartland of Palghar, our 16 acres were shaped by patience, care, and a deep love for the land. Every corner here tells a story — from the 700 coconut trees standing tall to the private lakes that mirror a sky you rarely get to see.

We are just over an hour from Mumbai, yet here you will feel something rare: stillness. Come as you are, and leave as you were always meant to feel.

The Land

700 Coconut Trees
Standing Tall

The coconut trees are not just trees — they are the original keepers of this land. Planted with intention, they line every path and frame every view. Walk beneath their canopy at dusk and you will understand why we call this place living.

The land breathes here. The birds know it. The wind through the palms carries a quiet that no city soundtrack can replicate. This is what we have chosen to protect and share.

The Lakes

Private Lakes That
Mirror the Sky

Three private lakes grace this land. At sunrise, they hold the sky in their still waters — a canvas that changes every hour. At sunset, they burn gold and amber, reflecting a world unhurried by time.

Sit by their edge and watch the herons fish. Take a boat out in the cool early hours. Or simply let the sound of water settle you in a way nothing else quite can. These lakes are Sanjivani's heartbeat.

"Every lake here mirrors a sky you rarely get to see."

Our cottages are not accommodations — they are an immersion. Built with natural materials and placed deliberately within the landscape, each one wakes up to birdsong and sleeps under an unpolluted sky full of stars.
